## Mobile Responsive

On mobile devices:
- Order cards stack vertically
- Contact info remains prominent
- Email/phone links work perfectly
- Easy to tap and call/email

## What You Can Do

1. **View all orders** - Scroll through the list
2. **See customer details** - Name, email, phone, address
3. **Contact customers** - Click email or phone
4. **Check order items** - See what they ordered
5. **View total amount** - Know the order value
6. **Track status** - See order progress

## Next Actions (Your Workflow)

1. **See new order** (PENDING badge)
2. **Click email or phone** to contact customer
3. **Discuss details**:
   - Confirm order items
   - Arrange payment method
   - Set delivery date
4. **Update status** to PROCESSING (manual for now)
5. **Receive payment**
6. **Fulfill order**
7. **Update status** to COMPLETED

## Tips

- Check dashboard regularly for new orders
- Respond to customers within 24 hours
- Keep customer info confidential
- Update order status as you progress
- Use the contact info to provide great service
